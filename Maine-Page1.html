Lobster in Maine is great! Maine lobsters are considered superior because they thrive in cold, clean ocean waters which produce a sweeter, more tender meat with less saltiness compared to lobsters from warmer regions; this is due to the cold temperatures preventing excess saltwater from permeating the lobster flesh, resulting in a high-quality, succulent taste. Additionally, Maine has strict regulations regarding lobster harvesting, ensuring sustainability and only catching lobsters at their prime size.
